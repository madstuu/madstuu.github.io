Basic Rules of Tennis

  • Players use a racket to hit the ball over the net to their opponent
  • A point is scored when the opponent fails to return the ball properly
  • Games are grouped into sets - the first to win 6 or 8 - winning by 2
  • The order goes game, set, match
  • Matches are best of 3 or 5 sets
